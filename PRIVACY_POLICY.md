# Privacy Policy for KLPS Pangire Learner

**Effective Date:** 2026-06-18

KLPS Pangire Learner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially primary school students. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our educational application (the "App").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).** 

The App is designed to be fully functional offline. All user progress, including:
- Earned Stars
- Unlocked Trophies and Achievements
- Game scores and completion status (e.g., words caught, problems solved)

are stored **locally** on your device using local storage. We do not maintain any servers to collect this data, nor do we track individual users.

## 2. Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important to us. Because our App is directed at primary school students:
- We do not require any account creation or login.
- We do not ask for any personal details such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
- We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking software that collects data on children.
- We do not serve advertisements within the App.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App may use bundled third-party assets (such as Tailwind CSS, Google Fonts, and Google Material Icons) which are packaged directly within the App to support 100% offline functionality. Because these assets are bundled locally, they do not make external network requests when you use the App.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the App operates offline and we do not collect contact information, any changes to this Privacy Policy will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document in future versions of the App.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
